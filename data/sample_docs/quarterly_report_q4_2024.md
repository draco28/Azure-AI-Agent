# TechCorp Q4 2024 Quarterly Business Report

## Executive Summary

Q4 2024 was a strong quarter for TechCorp, with total revenue reaching $48.7M, a 12% increase over Q3 2024 ($43.5M) and 23% year-over-year growth from Q4 2023 ($39.6M). Operating margin improved to 18.2%, up from 16.5% in Q3.

Key achievements:
- DataSync Enterprise customer count grew from 342 to 418 (22% growth)
- Launched DataSync 4.2 with real-time collaboration features
- Expanded APAC sales team from 8 to 15 members
- Achieved SOC 2 Type II certification

## Customer Metrics

- Total customers: 2,847 (up from 2,651 in Q3)
- Enterprise customers: 418 (up from 342 in Q3)
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR): 118%
- Customer churn rate: 2.1% (down from 2.8% in Q3)
- Average contract value (Enterprise): $58,600/year
- Net Promoter Score (NPS): 62 (up from 57 in Q3)

Top new enterprise customers signed in Q4:
1. Meridian Healthcare - 3-year, $420K contract
2. Atlas Financial Group - 2-year, $280K contract
3. Nordic Energy Partners - 3-year, $350K contract

## Engineering & Product

### DataSync 4.2 Release (shipped November 15)

Major features delivered:
- Real-time collaborative editing for documents
- Advanced search with natural language queries
- Automated compliance tagging based on content analysis
- Custom workflow builder for document approval chains

### Platform Reliability

- Uptime: 99.97% (above 99.95% SLA)
- Average API response time: 145ms (down from 168ms in Q3)
- Zero P0 (critical) incidents during the quarter
- 14 P1 incidents resolved with average resolution time of 2.3 hours
