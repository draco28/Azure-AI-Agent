Employee Handbook - TechCorp Inc.

Section 1: Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with prior manager approval. Remote work arrangements must be documented through HR within 5 business days of approval. Employees are expected to maintain regular working hours (9 AM - 5 PM local time) and be available for video calls during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM).

All remote workers must have a dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum 25 Mbps download speed). The company will provide a one-time $500 stipend for home office setup upon approval of remote work arrangement.

Section 2: Leave Policy

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per calendar year. PTO accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month. Unused PTO may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.

Sick leave is provided separately at 10 days per year and does not carry over. Employees must notify their manager before 9 AM on the day of absence.

Parental leave: Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of paid leave. Secondary caregivers receive 6 weeks of paid leave.

Section 3: Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice per year — mid-year (June) and end-of-year (December). Reviews are based on a 360-degree feedback model including self-assessment, peer reviews, and manager evaluation.

Rating scale: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement, Unsatisfactory.

Employees rated "Exceeds Expectations" are eligible for a bonus of up to 15% of base salary. Promotions are considered during the end-of-year review cycle only.

Section 4: Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurrence. Receipts are required for all expenses over $25. The following categories have pre-approved limits:

- Meals during business travel: $75 per day
- Transportation: Actual cost with receipt
- Client entertainment: $200 per event (manager approval required)
- Professional development: $2,000 per year per employee

All reimbursement requests are processed through the Finance portal and paid within 10 business days.
